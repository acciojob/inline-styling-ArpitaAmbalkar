Flowers photos

Flowers first evolved between 150 and 190 million years ago during the Jurassic period and represent a key evolutionary advancement, allowing flowering plants to dominate many ecosystems due to their efficient reproductive mechanisms.

Roses pictures

A rose is a woody perennial flowering plant of the genus Rosa in the family Rosaceae, or the flower it bears. There are over three hundred species and tens of thousands of cultivars, with plants ranging from compact miniature roses to climbers that can reach seven meters in height.
